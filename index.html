This is the official website of Corrupted Multiplayer a minecraft server. This server is for bedrock. It is based on team system. There are multiple teams and each team consists of 4 players. You can make your own team by registering on this website. The requirements for creating a team are :

  1. 4 members are required
  2. Team name is necessary
  3. Team leader is necessary
  4. Team must be registered
